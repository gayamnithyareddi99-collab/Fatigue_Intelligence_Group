The Platform

Every Layer of Fatigue Intelligence Group's Safety Intelligence

Fatigue Intelligence Group is a cloud-native SaaS platform built on a modular architecture real-time dashboards, biometric API integration, proprietary fatigue risk data model, and UK GDPR-compliant infrastructure designed to serve high-risk UK sectors without requiring in-house safety data science teams.

Module 01

AI Fatigue Risk Scoring

Machine learning models that calculate a real-time Fatigue Risk Score for every worker on shift factoring in sleep patterns, shift length, circadian phase, workload intensity, and historical incident data.

Each worker develops a unique Fatigue Profile a combination of personal risk tolerance, shift history, and physiological response patterns. This methodology is proprietary and creates highly defensible, legally admissible fatigue risk evidence.
Module 02

Predictive Shift Intelligence

Automatically diagnoses high-risk shift patterns excessive consecutive nights, insufficient rest windows, and dangerous shift rotation sequences before rotas are published.

Trained on UK sector-specific incident data and HSE fatigue research. The system self-calibrates risk thresholds by role and sector, providing a robust audit trail for regulatory inspections.
Module 03

Biometric Integration Layer

Integrates with wearable devices, access control systems, and telematics to enrich the fatigue risk model with objective physiological data heart rate variability, reaction time, and alertness indicators.

Generates comprehensive fatigue intelligence reports that link biometric markers to operational performance, providing safety managers with the objective evidence base previously unavailable to non-enterprise organisations.
Module 04

Compliance Automation Engine

Automatically checks every shift and rota against Working Time Regulations, sector-specific rest requirements, and internal fatigue policies flagging breaches before they become liabilities.

Performance-Weighted Risk Evolution ensures high-risk patterns are automatically escalated. New rotas are validated against established safe scheduling patterns before deployment, reducing compliance risk to near zero.
Module 05

Sector Benchmarking Network

A federated learning architecture enabling anonymised fatigue risk data from thousands of UK organisations to continuously improve predictive models without sharing sensitive workforce data.

Fatigue patterns from logistics hubs in the Midlands can improve safety intelligence for healthcare trusts in Manchester. Provides sector-specific benchmarks allowing organisations to compare their fatigue risk profile against industry standards.
Module 06

Incident Forensics Engine

Virtually reconstructs shift conditions at the time of any incident to determine the fatigue contribution providing legally defensible root cause analysis for HSE investigations, insurance claims, and internal reviews.

Human-in-the-Loop Safety Index provides a composite measure of organisational fatigue governance quality, converting manual incident review into an automated, evidence-based safety intelligence process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Fatigue Intelligence Group

What is fatigue risk management and why does it matter for UK employers?

+
Fatigue risk management is the process of identifying, monitoring, and mitigating the safety risks posed by worker fatigue in shift-based and high-demand roles. UK employers have a duty of care under the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974 and the Working Time Regulations 1998 to ensure workers are not placed at risk from fatigue. Fatigue Intelligence Group operationalises that duty turning it from a paper policy into a live, evidence-based safety system.

Which sectors does Fatigue Intelligence Group serve?

+
Our platform is designed for any UK organisation with shift-based workforces or high-demand roles: logistics and supply chain (warehouse, transport, distribution), healthcare (NHS Trusts, care homes, emergency services), manufacturing and industrial, construction, and security services. Phase 1 focuses on logistics and healthcare the two sectors with the highest documented fatigue-related incident rates in the UK.

Is Fatigue Intelligence Group compliant with UK data protection requirements?

+
Yes. Fatigue Intelligence Group is architecturally designed to comply with UK GDPR, ICO guidance on health data processing, and ISO 45003 (Psychological Health & Safety at Work). Biometric and health data is processed under explicit consent frameworks, with full data subject rights built into the platform. All fatigue reports are generated in formats suitable for HSE inspection, employment tribunal, and insurance purposes.

More Questions

Advanced Platform Questions

How does the biometric integration work?

+
Fatigue Intelligence Group integrates with leading wearable devices via open APIs including Garmin Health, Fitbit for Enterprise, Whoop, and access control systems. Data flows securely into the fatigue risk model under explicit worker consent frameworks, with full data subject rights and anonymisation controls. No biometric data is shared with employers without worker consent; the platform outputs a risk score, not raw physiological data.

Can Fatigue Intelligence Group integrate with our existing HR or scheduling systems?

+
Yes. Our platform offers pre-built integrations with SAP HR, Workday, BambooHR, Kronos Workforce Management, and leading rota platforms. For bespoke systems, our RESTful API allows integration with any HR, scheduling, or ERP platform. The Risk Intelligence and Safety Sovereign plans include full integration support as part of onboarding.

What does an HSE-ready forensic report look like?

+
Our forensic incident reports reconstruct the exact shift conditions fatigue scores, rest periods, cumulative hours, biometric markers if available at the time of any safety incident or near-miss. Reports are structured to meet HSE investigation standards, suitable for RIDDOR submissions, employment tribunal proceedings, and insurance claims. Reports are generated in PDF and structured data formats, exportable on demand.
